A letter I never sent to myself

Shared by Leila

I spent years waiting for someone to tell me I was enough. One day, I realized the words had to come from me first.

I had been kind to others while being cruel to myself. I had forgiven people who hurt me while punishing myself for being tired.

So this is the letter I never sent:

You did your best. You survived. You kept going. And that matters.

You are not behind. You are becoming.
