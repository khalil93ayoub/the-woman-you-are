The Wife You Are

Love is not built in grand moments. It is built in ordinary ones.

In patience after difficult days. In support during uncertain times. In choosing the same person again and again, even when life becomes challenging.

A wife is often seen as a partner. But what people do not always see is everything that comes with that role.

The encouragement. The sacrifices. The understanding. The quiet acts of love that happen when nobody is watching.

You help carry burdens that are not yours alone. You create warmth where there was none.

You turn a place into a home and moments into memories.

And through it all, you continue giving pieces of your heart. Not because you have to, but because you choose to.

And that choice, made day after day, is one of the purest forms of love there is.

Thank you for every unseen act of kindness. Thank you for every moment of strength. Thank you for every day you chose love.
