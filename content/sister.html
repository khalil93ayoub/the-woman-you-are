The Sister You Are

You were one of life's first gifts.

Before the world became complicated, before responsibilities arrived, before adulthood changed everything, you were there.

You shared childhood memories, inside jokes, secrets, and dreams that only the two of you would ever fully understand.

You stood beside people during their best moments, and often during their worst.

Sometimes your love looked like protection. Sometimes it looked like honesty. Sometimes it looked like simply staying when others walked away.

A sister becomes something rare in this world.

A person who remembers who you were before life changed you.

A person who knows your story without needing an explanation.

You may not always hear it, but your presence matters more than you know.

You have been a comfort, a confidant, a friend, and a home.

And if nobody has told you recently: thank you for staying.

Thank you for caring. Thank you for being one of the people who makes life feel a little less lonely.
