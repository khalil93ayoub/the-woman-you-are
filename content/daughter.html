The Daughter You Are

You entered this world carrying hope before you even understood what hope was.

Someone looked at you and imagined a future filled with possibilities, dreams, and happiness.

As you grew, the world began telling you who you should become. How you should look. How you should act. How you should live.

But your worth was never hidden inside other people's expectations.

Your worth has always been yours.

You are not valuable because you are perfect. You are valuable because you are you.

Because of your heart. Your dreams. Your courage to keep moving forward, even when the path is uncertain.

There will be people who underestimate you. There will be people who doubt you.

Do not become one of them.

Because somewhere inside you is a strength greater than you realize.

And the world becomes brighter every time a daughter chooses to believe in herself.
